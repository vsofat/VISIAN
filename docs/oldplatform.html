Our Platform

We, Vishwaa and Julian, believe that our goals are in the best interests of the student body. Some of our goals might seem to be far-fetched, and it is fair to say we are trying to accomplish a lot within one year. While we acknowledge this, we think it is of greater value to recognize a variety of issues that we can potentially address. This provides options and flexibility to best serve the needs of the student body. We guarantee that we will be unwavering in our advocacy for these policies and that we will always prioritize student voice and opinion. We hope to reach new heights and set the standards for future Student Union administrations.
