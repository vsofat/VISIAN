Deven Maheshwari

Hi! I’m Deven and I’m a campaign manager for VISIAN 2020! This year, I have been the head of project management for the freshman caucus where I have been meeting with the administration to have policies enacted to benefit Stuy students. Outside of the SU, I am a part of the Stuyvesant Boys’ Basketball and Volleyball teams and was a member of Stuy SAYA. I really like listening and playing music and I know how to play guitar and double bass. A fun fact about me is that I like biking in the city.

I have faith in Vishwaa and Julian to lead the SU and have their proposals be implemented in our daily lives. They both have a lot of experience in SU and have already been introducing change such as the updated cell phone policy. Vishwaa and Julian also have specific plans for the future in getting their policies passed and their effects on the student body so the most people possible can benefit. Vishwaa and Julian are both hardworking and eager candidates that will work relentlessly to have the student body’s voices heard to make Stuy a stronger community for everyone.
