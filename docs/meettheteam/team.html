Meet the team

With a combined half a decade of experience in the SU, we have spent our Stuyvesant careers working to create meaningful change for the student body. Serving as Freshman and Sophomore Caucus President, and currently, as SU Vice President, Vishwaa has fought for numerous policy changes, including changes to the cell phone policy, homework policy, PE uniform policy, and the creation of PSAL frees. Vishwaa has also worked with the External Affairs Department to launch a Sexual Misconduct course for all Freshmen and fought hard with the PA and administration to replace Stuyvesant’s escalators. For the past two years, Julian has been leading the External Affairs Department of the Student Union. He represents Stuyvesant on the Manhattan Borough Student Advisory Council (BSAC), the Chancellor’s Student Advisory Council (CSAC), and the Youth Technical Assistance Center on Addressing Disproportionality (YTAC-D), where he works with students from around the city to coordinate school-level and DOE-level policy change.
