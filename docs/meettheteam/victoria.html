Victoria Wong

Hi! I’m Victoria, one of Vishwaa’s and Julian’s campaign managers. I love singing and acting, which is why I’m a really active member of the Stuyvesant Theater Community (STC). I’ve also been in the cast of SING! for the past three years and I sing in Stuy A Cappella. Besides performing, I also love helping others. I’m one of the Big Sib Chairs for the 2019-2020 school year and I help run Stuy Summer Tutoring, an organization that works with libraries around the city to help elementary school students improve their ELA and math skills. A fun fact about me is that I love hot Cheetos, and I eat them with chopsticks (it’s the only way to avoid staining your fingers!).

I really believe in Vishwaa’s and Julian’s ability to lead the Student Union, and you should too! You can’t deny that they have the most previous SU knowledge and experience, and their proposed ideas for the coming school year are not only going to improve students’ lives, they’re also grounded in specific plans. I know that they’re both extremely passionate about our school and that they’ll work tirelessly to make it a better and happier place!
