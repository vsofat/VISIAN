TRUE GROWTH AS A COMMUNITY.

To us, leadership is about inclusivity, transparency, and accessibility.

The Student Union must be representative of student voice open to working with all students. We have a strong push for transparency and communication to make your input and feedback our number one priority. This coming year if we work together, we can witness real change. Are you in?

Inclusivity

We understand that constructing a truly representative and effective Student Union requires for us to listen, understand and incorporate student feedback. No student should every feel left out or uncomfortable speaking with their student government.

Transparency

The student body has every right to know what the Student Union is up to. We strive to have full transparency with the student body and to use communication to not only keep everyone in the know, but to hold ourselves accountable to our goals.

Accessibility

We want each student to be able to reach out to us and express their discontentments. We plan to make ourselves availabel via email, social media and our chat bots but also through Q/A forums. We want to make it easier for you to contact us but also for you to understand us.
